Smart Solutions for Common Farming Problems

Technology-driven tools to make farming more productive, profitable, and sustainable

Soil Testing Labs Finder

  • Helps farmers easily find soil testing labs near them
  • Encourages soil testing before sowing, improving crop planning and yield

Crop Recommendation

  • Gives personalized crop suggestions based on the farmer's land and climate
  • Prevents poor crop choices, leading to better productivity and profit

Gamified Growth Monitoring

  • Makes daily monitoring of crops fun and engaging
  • Rewards good practices, increasing farmer motivation and better crop health

Plant Disease Detection

  • Farmers can click a picture of a sick plant leaf to get instant disease diagnosis
  • Saves time and money by recommending correct and minimal pesticide use
  • Helps in reducing crop loss due to late identification of diseases

Crop Price Prediction

  • Provides future price trends of crops in nearby markets
  • Helps farmers decide what to grow and when to sell for maximum profit

Video Call with Experts

  • Farmers get easy access to agri-scientists and specialists for doubts or advice
  • Reduces dependence on unreliable local sources or middlemen

E-commerce Marketplace

  • Farmers earn coins during crop growth tracking, which they can redeem for seeds, tools, fertilizers, etc
  • Encourages sustainable farming practices through reward system

Weather Alerts & Forecasts

  • Helps farmers make better decisions on irrigation, spraying, harvesting, etc
  • Prevents damage from sudden rain, frost, or extreme heat with timely alerts
